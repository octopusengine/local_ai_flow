---

### DYNAMIC/PRIVATE PORTS
(49152–65535)

Used for client-side connections and temporary communications.  
Assigned dynamically by the operating system.

---

### QUICK FACTS
- TCP (Transmission Control Protocol) – Connection-oriented, reliable.
- UDP (User Datagram Protocol) – Connectionless, faster.
- Well-Known Ports are fixed and standardized.
- Dynamic Ports are used temporarily by applications/clients.
